Procurement Scenario

You are representing a supplier company that has been invited by management to participate in an electronic reverse auction (e-auction).

The objective is to win a production contract for supplying 10,000 aluminum casings for automotive control modules. However, winning the contract alone is not sufficient; your goal is to secure the contract under conditions that allow your company to generate profit.

The buying firm is an industrial company in the automotive sector. Management has emphasized that this auction is highly competitive and strategically important.

The auction starts at a price of €200,000. Bids must be submitted downward. The supplier with the lowest valid offer will be awarded the contract.

Your internal production costs are uncertain and lie somewhere between €35,000 and €50,000. The exact cost level is not known to you at the time of bidding.

Management has announced that your performance in the auction may influence an additional bonus payment.
